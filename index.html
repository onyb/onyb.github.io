About Me

I am currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering from International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Bhubaneswar. I hail from the steel city of Rourkela in Odisha, but recently moved in to Kolkata with my parents.

During my undergraduate career, I have had the oppurtunity to work with amazing research institutes and organizations like CERN; GNU Octave; Harish-Chandra Research Institute (HRI), Allahabad; and Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER), Kolkata. So far it has been quite an interesting journey, and I look up to an equally challenging one in future. You can find some of the details on my CV.

In my first two years as an undergrad, I made some significant contributions to open source software projects during Google Summer of Code 2013 and 2014. I have not been very active in recent times, though I like to release much of what I write with liberal open source licences.

One of my research interests is Quantum Computation, which I have been pursuing for quite some time. I am more inclined towards algorithms which work using principles of Quantum Mechanics, though I am equally strong (or weak) in Quantum Information Theory as well. Lately, I have been engaged with some exciting software projects, and working on polishing my CS fundamentals.
