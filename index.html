                                   About me

I am Ani, a Software Engineer based in Paris.  I work at [Ledger], on the
[Vault] team, building institutional-grade cryptocurrency self-custody.

I'm deeply interested in programming as a craft; i.e. building products
that people <3, powered by </> that's delightful to read.

My current interests include cryptocurrencies, decentralized finance, and
programmable money; largely revolving around [Bitcoin] and [Ethereum]. I
have a passive interest in quantum algorithms, and weather forecasting.

To know more about my professional work, check out my [CV].  For a more
human-friendly version, check out my [Timeline].
