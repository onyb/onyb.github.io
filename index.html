Hello World!

I am Ani, a Software Engineer based in Paris. I work at Ledger, on the Vault team, building institutional-grade cryptocurrency self-custody.


I'm deeply interested in programming as a craft; i.e. building products that people fall in with, powered by that's delightful to read.

My current interests revolve around foundational topics underpinning Bitcoin and Ethereum, notably applied cryptography and cryptoeconomics. I also have a passive interest in quantum algorithms, and weather forecasting, thanks to some stints I did in the past. In my spare time, I like reading about and internalizing mental models.


To know more about my professional work, check out my CV. For a more human-friendly version with interesting anecdotes, head over to my Timeline. And while you're still here, why don't you check out some of my hand-picked projects below?
